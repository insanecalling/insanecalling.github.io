Iceland itinerary

Here is our day to day itinerary. We went clockwise on our ring road trip. We had 15 full days in Iceland as we arrived at midnight on day 1 and left early morning on day 16.

In addition to the ring road, we explored the following areas: Snæfellsnes peninsula, Tröllaskagi peninsula, central highlands (Askja tour) and the Westman islands. We spent a day in each of these places.
